Chicken

Description

Oven baked chicken seasoned with 'Slap Ya Mamas' seasoning

Ingredients

  • Boneless Chicken Breast
  • Slap Ya Mama seasoning
  • Canola Oil

Steps

  1. Season
  2. Pre-Heat Oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit
  3. Bake Chicken for 40 minutes
  4. Cover Chicken for 10 minutes with foil
